# SkyLine Airlines – Policies & FAQ

## Refund Policy

**Full Refund:**
Passengers are eligible for a full refund if they cancel their flight **more than 48 hours before departure**. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.

**Partial Refund (Cancellation Fee):**
Cancellations made **24–48 hours before departure** are eligible for a 50% refund. A $75 cancellation fee applies.

**No Refund:**
Cancellations made **less than 24 hours before departure** are non-refundable. However, passengers may receive a travel credit valid for 12 months.

**Non-Refundable Tickets:**
Promotional and Basic Economy fares are non-refundable under all circumstances. Travel credits are not available for these fares.

---

## Flight Change Policy

**Free Changes:**
Passengers in Business Class or with Flex fares may change their flight **for free**, up to 2 hours before departure.

**Change Fees:**
- Economy passengers: $50 change fee applies per change
- Changes made within 24 hours of departure: $100 fee
- Same-day flight changes (standby): $30 fee if available

**How to Change:**
Call our customer support at 1-800-SKYLINE or use the online portal.

---

## Baggage Policy

**Carry-On:** One carry-on bag (22" x 14" x 9") and one personal item are included in all fares.

**Checked Bags:**
- Basic Economy: No checked bags included ($35 per bag)
- Economy: First bag free, second bag $45
- Business Class: Two checked bags included

**Overweight Fees:**
- 51–70 lbs: $75 per bag
- 71–100 lbs: $150 per bag

---

## Seat Selection

**Economy:** Seat selection available for $15–$45 depending on seat location.
**Exit Row / Extra Legroom:** $35–$75 per segment.
**Business Class:** Complimentary seat selection included.

---

## Check-In Policy

- **Online Check-In:** Opens 24 hours before departure, closes 1 hour before.
- **Airport Check-In:** Available 3 hours before departure.
- **Boarding:** Gates close 15 minutes before departure. Passengers must be at the gate 30 minutes before.

---

## Special Assistance

Passengers requiring wheelchair assistance, special meals, or other accommodations should contact us at least **48 hours before departure** at 1-800-SKYLINE or accessibility@skylineair.com.

---

## Frequent Flyer Program – SkyMiles

- Earn 1 mile per dollar spent on Economy tickets.
- Earn 2 miles per dollar spent on Business tickets.
- Miles expire after 18 months of account inactivity.
- **Elite Status:** Silver (25,000 miles/year), Gold (50,000 miles/year), Platinum (100,000 miles/year).
